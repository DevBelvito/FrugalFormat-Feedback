# Privacy Policy for FrugalFormat

**Last Updated:** July 20, 2026

FrugalFormat ("the App") is a desktop file conversion utility built with a strict commitment to user privacy. This policy explains our "offline-first" approach and how your data is protected.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**FrugalFormat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, telemetry, or analytics.**

The Application operates entirely offline on your local machine. We do not track how you use the Application, which features you interact with, or any identifying information about your device.

## 2. File Processing
All file manipulations—including PDF conversions, media transcoding, image formatting, and Optical Character Recognition (OCR)—are executed locally on your device's hardware.
* **Zero Uploads:** At no point are your files, documents, or media uploaded to external servers, cloud providers, or third-party services.
* **Local Storage:** The Application only reads and writes files to the directories you explicitly choose on your local drives.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Network Access
FrugalFormat does not require an active internet connection to function. However, the Application contains optional links to external platforms for community support and feedback:
* **GitHub:** For submitting bug reports and feature requests.
* **Ko-fi:** For voluntarily supporting the development of the Application.

If you choose to click these links, your web browser will open these respective platforms. FrugalFormat does not share any data with these platforms. Please refer to their individual privacy policies for information on how they handle your data when you visit their sites.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
If our practices change in the future (for example, if new features require optional network permissions), we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly communicate those changes within the Application's release notes. However, our commitment to never collecting your personal data or uploading your files will remain absolute.
